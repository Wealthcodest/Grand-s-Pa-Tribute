Here is an interest time-line of facts about my awesome grandfather

Mr Ogbobor is my grandfather from my maternal home. He is very tall, 6ft 3inches, slender in size and quite a man of few words.
I didn't spend much time with him but I enjoyed every moment spent around me. He left quite an impression in my memory that sure lives with me always.

  • My Ogbobor is a native of ughelli-south LGA of the Delta Region, Nigeria
  • He was a trailer driver by profession
  • By reason of his job, he is quite a traveller and has visited every nooks and crannies of our Mother country, Nigeria
  • He was a Lagosian and a people person
